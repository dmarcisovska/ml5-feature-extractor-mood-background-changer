Mood Background Changer

Train the model below to detect your mood.

  1. Make your best exaggerated smile and press the smile button 10 or more times.
  2. Make your best exaggerated frown and press the frown button 10 or more times.
  3. Click the train button once.
  4. Wait a few seconds for the model to train itself.
  5. The model will now change background based on if you are smiling or frowning.
  6. If you like you can use objects or different gestures instead of a smile or frown.
